About

Hello! I'm Kenisha, an aspiring Creative Director, author, and travel enthusiast studying at the University of Miami,

My first love was creativity;

At an early age, my definition of fun consisted of exploring different forms of art: from designing to writing, and visual arts. My lifelong relationship with creativity has transformed my adolescent interests into career goals.

For the past six years, I've been building my portfolio with Strikingly for posting my photography and literary work. Crafting a personal brand while connecting my life experiences with various applications, such as Adobe Creative Studio, has given a unique perspective on social media management and content creation.

Currently working towards a bachelor degree at the University of Miami with a double major in journalism and creative advertising, I work as an editor for the 2023 Ibis Yearbook in the academic section and Gravity Magazine. Prior to transferring to UM, I studied at Montclair State University with a major in hospitality, sports, events, and travel while being an intern for the Disney College Program in 2022.

Outside of college and the office, I enjoy creating YouTube vlogs, indulging in fashion, and traveling! Exploring the cities I've visited has been the highlight of navigating through my early adulthood years...
