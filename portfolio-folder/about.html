About

Hello! I'm Kenisha, an aspiring Creative Director studying at the University of Miami.

My first love was creativity;

At an early age, my definition of fun consisted of exploring different forms of art: from designing to writing, and visual arts. My lifelong relationship with creativity has transformed my adolescent interests into career goals.

For the past six years, I've been building my portfolio with Strikingly for posting my photography and literary work.

Currently working towards a bachelor's degree at the University of Miami with a major in journalism and creative advertising, I work as an editor for the 2023 Ibis Yearbook in the academic section and Gravity Magazine.

Outside of college and the office, I enjoy creating YouTube vlogs, indulging in fashion, and traveling! Experiencing life while visiting cities around the world has been the highlight of navigating through my adulthood.

I'm excited to see where my next destination will be and what the next project may entail...
